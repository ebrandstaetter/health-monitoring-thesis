This diploma thesis was made in cooperation with the austrian software company Hello Again.
Our goal was to develop a Health Monitoring Dashboards that helps their employees to keep track of the health status of every Loyalty App.
The dashboard shows if an app is available and healthy, which problems may occur in the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, and how many open tickets have been created regarding this app.
On the technical side the dashboard was developed by using Vue.js in the frontend after we designed it with Figma, in the backend we used FastAPI with Uvicorn and Pydantic and is connected with extern services like Google Play Developer API, die App Store Connect API, HubSpot and Asana.
We developed the dashboard in our own Git-Branch and in the end our code got transferred in the company's project.
